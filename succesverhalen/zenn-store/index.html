CHALLENGE

Build a high-volume UGC pipeline for a precise, regulated product

Zenn Store's ads had to be clear, compliant, and specific. That meant creators needed to follow tight scripts, deliver with charisma, and help the team test lots of variations without bogging down in casting, contracts, and back-and-forth.

IMPLEMENTATION

Scale scripted UGC with repeatable testing

Zenn Store used UGC.NL to move quickly from casting to production to iteration. The team leaned on UGC.NL's Top Ad Performers to avoid wasted time, prioritized creators who matched their 35-55 audience, and ran a disciplined testing system built around hooks and multiple creators per script. Daryll leaned into volume: they needed a lot of quantity to find winners, fast.

Targeted creator selection

Zenn Store's target audience skewed older, so Daryll typically selected creators 30+. Instead of browsing endlessly, he started with UGC.NL's Top Ad Performers, then filtered by one main factor: charisma that made people stop scrolling.

Scripted briefs, executed cleanly

Because the product demanded precision, Zenn Store's briefs were highly scripted. Creators still delivered well: they read the script, followed it closely, and executed at a high level, without the team needing to train every creator from scratch.

Hook variation testing at volume

Zenn Store's core method was structured and repeatable: one script, four hook variations, and at least five creators. This approach helped narrow down what worked, reduce wasted effort, and keep performance from relying on luck.

Formats that matched how people watch

Zenn Store found that simple, low-production UGC performed best. TikTok-style hooks outperformed brand-first intros, especially early on when audiences didn't know Zenn Store yet. The team also leaned into a more square format, rather than fully vertical, as a consistent winner.

POSITIVE OUTCOMES

Higher CTR, longer watch time, and a smoother workflow

Performance lift. CTR improved from 2.98% to 3.63%, average play time rose from 00:06 to 00:11, and CPA decreased by 15%.

UGC.NL drove the winners. About 75% of Zenn Store's videos came from UGC.NL, including most of their top performers.

Less overhead, more output. UGC.NL removed the time-consuming work of finding actors, handling contracts, and chasing people down, so the team could focus on creative performance instead of logistics.

Reliable creator quality. Daryll reported that about 95% of UGC.NL creators he worked with were really good, with very few issues across high volume.

FUTURE PLANS AND RECOMMENDATIONS

More platform coverage, expanded testing

Zenn Store planned to keep scaling UGC.NL as a primary content source while expanding on Meta and TikTok, as well as other channels. The team would continue running high-volume hook tests, relying on Top Ad Performers to move fast without sacrificing quality.
